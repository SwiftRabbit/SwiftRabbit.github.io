Blog, you shall be written!

2017-10-08

I’ve been told by a few people, and I’ve been telling myself the same for quite a while now, you should write a blog. By the way, my name is Leopold, and I’m a dev. I am doing iOS Development for the most part.

There are already quite a few blogs/articles out there that do an amazing job. Most of them are quite interesting, with examples quite often, sometimes they are fun too (my favourites one usually, thanks KrakenDev). I will do my best to live up to them as the time passes and if I find interesting things to share with the community.

Why a blog?

In the past I tried quite a few times explaining abstract concepts to colleagues. I am among the people that work by example. Sometimes it’s not just about understanding what you can do with a language or tools. Sometimes it’s first about understanding the challenges/problems you are trying to overcome. Understanding helps you find elegant solutions.

For example, I was only able to understand the advantages of MVVM when I saw/understood how it was helping to reuse components. Before that MVVM was to me a beautiful abstract concept that did not hold any real value. Same thing can be said when I saw how the coordinator pattern was solving the bloated AppDelegate. Sometimes, you have to get your hands dirty to actually understand how solutions make you more efficient in real life.

Code Human > Code Monkey

In this blog I will try to have 5 guidelines:

  • You shall give (/alternative) solutions to problems with struct
  • You shall give examples/sample projects/walkthrough with explanations
  • You shall give pictures and diagrams when you can, because a picture is worth a thousant words
  • You shall be accessible for the weak, the strong and the unowned
